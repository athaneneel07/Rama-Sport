How to Connect Playo/Khelomore SMS

Because 3rd party booking apps send random text formats, the system cannot safely guess the date/time automatically. Follow these steps to send the SMS to your dashboard, and then you can click "Manual Booking" to officially block the slot.

  1. Download the MacroDroid app on your Android phone.
  2. Create a new Macro. Set Trigger to "SMS Received" (Select the Playo/Khelomore sender name).
  3. Set Action to "HTTP Request". Method: POST.
  4. URL: Paste your Google Script URL.
  5. Content Type: application/x-www-form-urlencoded
  6. Add Parameters: type=macrodroid and sms_text=[SMS Message Content Text]
  7. Now, whenever an SMS comes, it will show up in the Bookings table for you to review and block manually!
